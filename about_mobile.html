Our Story: The Heart of Redeeming Time Today

Discover the journey, values, and vision that drive our commitment to empowering individuals and fostering positive change in our community.

From Vision to Reality

Redeeming Time Today was born from a profound vision shared within the challenging environment of Rahway State Prison. Our CEO, alongside Reverend Rufus McClendon and a group of dedicated inmates, envisioned a pathway to holistic growth encompassing mental, physical, and spiritual well being.

This powerful idea, conceived in adversity, was carried beyond prison walls upon our CEO's release in 2002. Redeeming Time Today was then formally established, transforming that initial spark into a beacon of hope. Our organization was founded on the principle that every individual deserves a chance to rejoin society with dignity, purpose, and comprehensive support, especially those navigating the difficult paths away from addiction, incarceration, and homelessness.

"Making the very most of your time [on earth], ...because the days are evil."

Ephesians 5:16 (AMP), The Guiding Word for Redeeming Time Today

Furthermore, a core tenet of Redeeming Time Today is our commitment to youth. We strive to guide young people towards making choices that build bright, fulfilling futures, steering them clear of decisions that could lead to shattered dreams and lost potential.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every individual with empathy, understanding, and a genuine desire to support their journey with Redeeming Time Today.

Empowerment

We believe in equipping people with the tools, resources, and confidence to take control of their lives and make positive changes.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging and mutual support, recognizing that collective strength is vital for individual and societal healing.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our interactions and programs at Redeeming Time Today.

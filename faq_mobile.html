Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Redeeming Time Today, our mission, programs, and how you can be a part of our community.

{/* FAQ Item 1 */}

Redeeming Time Today is dedicated to empowering individuals and communities to maximize their potential through the wise and purposeful use of time. We provide transformative resources, dedicated mentorship, and impactful programs that foster personal development, community service, and meaningful engagement. Our aim is to inspire and equip every person to live with intention, making each moment a stepping stone towards a brighter, better future, particularly for those overcoming addiction, incarceration, and homelessness.

{/* FAQ Item 2 */}

If you or someone you know needs support, please visit our Get Involved page and fill out the "Need Support? Reach Out to Us" form. You can also contact us directly via email at robert@redeemingthetimetoday.com. We are here to listen and connect you with available resources and programs.

{/* FAQ Item 3 */}

We welcome passionate individuals to volunteer their time and skills! Please visit our Get Involved page and complete the "Volunteer Your Time & Skills" form. You can specify your areas of interest, and our team will reach out to discuss current opportunities with Redeeming Time Today.

{/* FAQ Item 4 */}

Your generous donations directly support Redeeming Time Today's programs and operational costs. This includes funding for community reintegration services, youth mentorship initiatives, recovery support programs, holistic development workshops, and essential resources for individuals and families in need. We are committed to transparency; you can find more details on our Donate page and in our financial reports.

{/* FAQ Item 5 */}

Redeeming Time Today was born from a shared vision within Rahway State Prison by our CEO and Rev. Rufus McClendon, along with inmates dedicated to holistic growth. Upon release in 2002, this vision was established as an organization to offer hope and support for those rejoining society. You can read more about our origins on our About Us page.

{/* FAQ Item 6 */}

Yes! Redeeming Time Today regularly hosts community events and online meetings. You can find the latest schedules and details on our Events page and our Meetings page. We encourage you to join us!

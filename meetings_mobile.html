Connect & Grow: Our Community Meetings

Join Redeeming Time Today for engaging online meetings designed to foster connection, share resources, and support personal growth within our community.

Why Join Our Meetings?

A Space for Support and Empowerment

Our regular online meetings at Redeeming Time Today offer a welcoming environment for individuals from all walks of life. Whether you are seeking guidance, wish to share your experiences, or simply want to connect with like minded individuals, these sessions are for you.

  • Build supportive relationships and network with community members.
  • Participate in discussions on relevant topics like personal development, recovery, and community action.
  • Gain insights, share wisdom, and find encouragement on your journey.
  • Discover resources and opportunities available through Redeeming Time Today and our partners.

Our Meeting Environment

To ensure a positive and respectful experience for everyone at Redeeming Time Today meetings, we uphold these simple guidelines:

  • Be respectful of all participants and their views.
  • Listen actively when others are speaking.
  • Maintain confidentiality regarding personal shares.
  • Engage constructively and supportively.

Our Journey of Redemption

Our CEO started what we now call Redeeming the Time Today, in a prison in Rahway, New Jersey with Rev. Rufus McClendon and a group of prisoners who shared the vision of helping anyone, anytime, anywhere to grow mentally, physically, and spiritually! Upon his release in 2002, he started Redeeming The Time on the other side of the walls!

Our mission is simple: to create a space where members of our community can find assistance as they regroup and rejoin society, navigating away from addictions, jails, prisons, homelessness, and institutions. We also work with our youth to prevent decisions that can destroy their lives and dreams.

"Redeeming the time, because the days are evil."
- Ephesians 5:16

Our Guiding Mission

Redeeming Time Today is dedicated to helping individuals and communities maximize their potential by using time wisely and purposefully. We provide resources, mentorship, and programs that encourage personal development, service, and meaningful engagement. Our mission is to inspire and equip people to live with intention, making every moment count for a better future.

How We Help

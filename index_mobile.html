Our Genesis

Born from a vision within walls, thriving beyond them.

From Rahway to Renewal

Redeeming The Time Today began in a New Jersey prison, a shared dream between our CEO, Rev. Rufus McClendon, and fellow inmates. It was a vision to empower anyone, anytime, anywhere to achieve holistic growth—mentally, physically, and spiritually.

Released in 2002, our CEO carried this vision forward, establishing Redeeming The Time beyond the prison walls. Today, we are a sanctuary for those seeking to rebuild, rejoin, and reclaim their lives from the shadows of addiction, incarceration, and homelessness.

"Making the very most of your time [on earth, recognizing and taking advantage of each opportunity and buying up the time], because the days are evil." Ephesians 5:16 (AMP)

Our Core Mission

Redeeming Time Today is dedicated to helping individuals and communities maximize their potential by using time wisely and purposefully. We provide resources, mentorship, and programs that encourage personal development, service, and meaningful engagement. Our mission is to inspire and equip people to live with intention, making every moment count for a better future.

Pathways to Progress

Re-entry & Reintegration

Guidance and support for individuals transitioning back into society from institutions, helping them find footing and purpose.

Our Genesis

About Redeeming Time Today

Born from a shared vision within Rahway State Prison, Redeeming Time Today was conceived by our CEO and Rev. Rufus McClendon with inmates dedicated to holistic growth. Since 2002, we are a beacon of hope, offering support for individuals rejoining society with dignity and purpose.

"Making the very most of your time [on earth], ...because the days are evil."

Ephesians 5:16 (AMP), The Guiding Word

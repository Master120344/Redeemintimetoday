Our Genesis

About Redeeming Time Today

Born from a shared vision within the walls of Rahway State Prison, Redeeming Time Today was conceived by our CEO and Rev. Rufus McClendon, alongside inmates dedicated to holistic growth. Since 2002, we have been a beacon of hope, offering support for individuals rejoining society with dignity and purpose.

Our Genesis

From Vision to Reality

Born from a shared vision within the walls of Rahway State Prison, Redeeming The Time Today (RTT) was conceived by our CEO and Rev. Rufus McClendon, alongside inmates dedicated to holistic growth—mentally, physically, and spiritually.

Upon release in 2002, this powerful vision transcended prison barriers. RTT was established to be a beacon of hope, offering a supportive space for individuals navigating paths away from addiction, incarceration, and homelessness. We champion the journey of rejoining society with dignity and purpose.

"Making the very most of your time [on earth], ...because the days are evil."

Ephesians 5:16 (AMP), The Guiding Word

We also invest in our youth, guiding them to make choices that build bright futures and avoid paths that could shatter their dreams.

Our Unwavering Mission

Redeeming Time Today is dedicated to empowering individuals and communities to maximize their potential through the wise and purposeful use of time. We provide transformative resources, dedicated mentorship, and impactful programs that foster personal development, community service, and meaningful engagement. Our aim is to inspire and equip every person to live with intention, making each moment a stepping stone towards a brighter, better future.
